Diary of a Wimpy Kid is a movie series based on the books by Jeff Kinney that follows the adventures and misadventures of Greg Heffley, a sixth grader who tries to fit in and survive middle school.
Along with his best friend Rowley, Greg faces various challenges such as bullies, popularity, crushes, and family issues.
The movies are a mix of comedy, drama, and family genres.
